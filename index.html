Who we are

Your Community Ambassadors

Hi! We're Teddi and Ed, the official Pokémon GO Community Ambassadors for the Nanaimo area. We organize raids, Community Days, and all kinds of meetups for in-game events — and we keep it welcoming for every kind of trainer.

Whether you just downloaded the game or you've been grinding since 2016, there's a spot for you here. No gatekeeping, no drama, just good people catching Pokémon together.
