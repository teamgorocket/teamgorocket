Tips & Tools

How our meetups work, the tools we rely on, and a stash of handy infographics for trainers of every level.

What to expect

Our meetup types

Our events vary by type. Some are group-focused and centered on a gathering spot, while others are designed for self-paced play wherever you are. Meetup details are always posted in Campfire — check there for the time and location.

Group-focused events

These have a clear gathering spot and a check-in window. Great for teaming up and tackling things together.

Examples: Raid Hour, Raid Day, Gigantamax Battles, GO Fest, GO Tour

Self-paced events

Well suited to playing individually or in small, informal groups at your own pace, on your own schedule.

Examples: Spotlight Hour, Hatch Day, Research Day, Max Monday

Campfire check-ins

How check-ins work

Pokémon GO events may run for several hours, but Campfire check-ins are for a specific time window listed in the meetup details. Checking in awards timed research with premium rewards tied to the event type — and more check-ins help our community earn swag to share.

Check in during the window

Each meetup lists its own check-in window. Be in the area and check in through Campfire during that time.

Claim your 500 XP

After checking in, don't forget to claim 500 XP to unlock your timed research with event-specific rewards.

Enjoy 8 hours of perks

For eight hours after checking in you get special field research, 3/4 egg hatch distance in a party, and party challenges with a higher chance at Tandemaus.

Local know-how

A few friendly tips

1

New here? Just show up.

You don't need to be high level or know the lingo. Pop into our Campfire group, say hi, and someone will help you find a raid or a Community Day meetup.

2

Arrive early for hosted events.

When we run meetups, getting there a few minutes early helps with check-ins and giveaways.

3

Catch the popular spots.

Maffeo Sutton Park and Beban Park are our regular stomping grounds. We'll also pop by Departure Bay or local malls depending on the event. Watch Campfire for where the group's meeting next.
